Mediterranean Green Plate
with Fresh Herbs

Savor the refreshing taste of the Mediterranean with this vibrant green plate. It features a mix of fresh greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions, complemented by the richness of feta cheese and the briny bite of olives. Topped with fresh herbs and a zesty vinaigrette, this dish is both nourishing and delicious—perfect for a light lunch or a side dish to your favorite meal.

Tags: Fresh •Light •Healthy

Allergens: Milk •Olives

**Produced in a facility that processes eggs, milk, fish, peanuts, sesame, shellfish, soy, tree nuts, and wheat.

Instructions

Ensure that your workspace is clean and organized. Gather all the necessary ingredients and utensils, such as a cutting board, knife, and salad bowl, before you begin preparing the dish.

Wash and dry the mixed greens, tomatoes, and cucumbers. Cut the tomatoes into wedges and slice the cucumbers thinly. Thinly slice the red onion.

In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions. Crumble the feta cheese over the top and add the olives.

In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined. Adjust seasoning to taste.

Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to coat. Serve the Mediterranean Green Plate immediately, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
