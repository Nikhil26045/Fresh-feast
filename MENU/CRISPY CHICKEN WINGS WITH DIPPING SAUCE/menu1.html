Chicken with Dipping Sauce

Total Time

30 min.

Calories:

750

Difficulty:

Easy

Description

Enjoy our crispy chicken tenders paired with a delectable dipping sauce that enhances every bite. Our chicken is tender and flavorful, coated in a light, crispy batter, and served with a choice of tangy or sweet dipping sauces. Perfect as a snack or a main dish, this combination offers a delightful crunch and an explosion of flavor.

Allergens

Contains: Eggs, Milk, Wheat, Soy. Produced in a facility that processes milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soybean.

Ingredients

Our Chicken with Dipping Sauce includes chicken tenders coated in a mix of flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs, seasoned with a blend of spices. The dipping sauce options include a tangy BBQ sauce and a creamy ranch dressing, both made with high-quality ingredients for a perfect pairing.
