Avocado Egg Toast
with a Hint of Lemon and Chili Flakes

Experience the perfect balance of creamy avocado and perfectly cooked egg with our Avocado Egg Toast! This simple yet elegant dish features ripe avocado spread over toasted bread, topped with a sunny-side-up egg. Seasoned with a touch of salt, pepper, and a sprinkle of chili flakes for a hint of heat, this toast is both nutritious and satisfying. Ideal for a quick breakfast or a light lunch, it combines rich flavors with a delightful texture for a truly enjoyable meal.

Tags: Healthy •Quick •Easy Prep

Allergens: Eggs •Wheat •Possible cross-contamination with other allergens

**Produced in a facility that processes eggs, wheat, milk, soy, and tree nuts.

Instructions

• Start by toasting your bread slices to your desired level of crispness. You can use a toaster or a toaster oven for this step.

• While the bread is toasting, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Optionally, add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra flavor.

• Heat a small non-stick pan over medium heat. Add a little oil or butter, and cook the egg to your preference (sunny-side up, poached, or scrambled). Season with salt and pepper.

• Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices. Place the cooked egg on top of the avocado spread. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes or any other desired toppings.

• Serve immediately while the toast is still warm. Enjoy your Avocado Egg Toast as a delicious and nutritious breakfast or snack.
