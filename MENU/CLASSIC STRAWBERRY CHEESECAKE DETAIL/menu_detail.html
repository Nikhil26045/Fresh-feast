Classic Cheesecake
with Fresh Berry Compote

Experience the ultimate indulgence with our classic cheesecake, featuring a rich and creamy filling nestled in a buttery graham cracker crust. Baked to perfection and topped with a luscious fresh berry compote, this dessert promises a delightful balance of sweetness and tang. Perfect for any occasion, it's a crowd-pleaser that's sure to leave a lasting impression.

Tags: Sweet • Creamy • Decadent

Allergens: Milk • Eggs • Wheat

**Produced in a facility that processes milk, eggs, fish, peanuts, sesame, shellfish, soy, tree nuts, and wheat.

Instructions

• Preheat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit (163 degrees Celsius). In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust.

• In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract, mixing until well combined. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.

• Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until the center is set and the edges are lightly golden. Let the cheesecake cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.

• For the berry compote, combine mixed berries and a bit of sugar in a saucepan. Cook over medium heat until berries are softened and the mixture has thickened. Allow to cool before topping the cheesecake.

• Once the cheesecake is chilled and the compote is ready, spread the berry compote over the top of the cheesecake. Serve chilled and enjoy!
